Order Triage & Management

A Microsoft-native way to turn emailed purchase orders into validated orders — automatically. AI reads the PO, checks it against your customers and products, and only true exceptions reach a person.
What it does Capabilities Before / after Outcomes Microsoft vs. Esker How it fits together See it

Today, customer purchase orders arrive by email — in the body or as a PDF — and are read and re-keyed by hand. Order Triage & Management does that work for you: an order lands, AI extracts the details, the system validates the customer and products, catches duplicates, and creates the order. Your team's attention goes only to the orders that genuinely need a human.

Microsoft-native Email & PDF intake AI extraction Works with or without Customer Service PCI-aware card handling

What it does

📥
Captures inbound orders
Watches your order mailboxes and picks up POs from the email body or a PDF attachment, automatically.
🤖
Reads the PO with AI
Extracts PO number, customer, ship-to, dates, and line items — with a confidence score on every field.
✅
Validates against your data
Matches the customer and each product, and flags duplicate POs — before anything is created.
📄
Side-by-side review
The original document next to the extracted fields, so a processor can confirm at a glance and click to index.
⚡
Creates the order
Clean orders are created automatically; only genuine exceptions are routed to a person — optionally as a Customer Service case.
🔒
Protects card data
If a PO carries a payment card, one click masks the number (and security code) and stores only the protected copy.
📊
Full visibility
A live dashboard of volume, processing time, exception rate, and SLA — the numbers operations can govern.
🧩
Fits your team's tools
Runs standalone, inside Dynamics 365 Customer Service, or as a simple model-driven app — same experience either way.

The change, in plain terms

⏱ Today — manual

  • A person opens every order email and PDF
  • Re-keys the PO by hand
  • Customer & product matched from memory
  • Duplicate orders can slip through
  • Hours of effort; errors creep in

⚡ With Order Triage

  • Email is detected automatically (body or PDF)
  • AI extracts the order fields
  • Auto-matched to your customers & products
  • Duplicate POs caught automatically
  • Minutes, not hours — exceptions only to people

How it fits together

Email lands → AI extracts → validated against your Dynamics 365 customers & products → clean orders are created automatically, and exceptions are handled by a person in the same triage workbench (with Copilot assist). A live dashboard tracks volume, processing time, and SLA. The workbench runs standalone or embedded in Customer Service.
